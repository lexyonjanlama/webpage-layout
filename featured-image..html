Featured Image Layout

Published on June 15, 2023 | 5 min read

A striking featured image creates immediate visual impact and sets the tone for your content. This layout combines the power of visuals with well-structured text.

The large hero image captures attention while the offset content area provides contrast and readability. This approach works particularly well for blog posts, news articles, and case studies.

Notice how the content container overlaps the image slightly, creating a layered effect that guides the eye naturally from the visual to the text.
