The Power of Single Column Layouts

This clean, focused approach puts content first by eliminating distractions and creating a natural reading flow.

Why It Works

The single column format matches how we naturally read - from top to bottom. This makes it ideal for long-form content like articles, blog posts, and documentation.

Mobile First

With no complex grids to rearrange, one-column layouts adapt perfectly to all screen sizes without requiring major structural changes.
